# Thock Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: TODO

Thock Studio is designed as a local Mac keyboard sound utility. It plays local sounds in response to keyboard events and does not collect personal data.

## Data Collection

Thock Studio does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Thock Studio does not store typed text, passwords, words, sentences, clipboard contents, screenshots, or document contents.

## Keyboard Access

Thock Studio requests macOS Input Monitoring permission so it can detect keyboard events and play local keyboard sounds while you type in other apps.

Keyboard events are used only for local sound playback. They are not sent to a server and are not used to reconstruct what you typed.

## Local Storage

Thock Studio stores app settings locally on your Mac, including selected sound pack, volume, mixer preferences, mute settings, and per-key sound assignments.

If you import a sound pack, Thock Studio copies that user-selected pack into local Application Support storage on your Mac.

## Network Activity

Thock Studio does not require an account and does not use a backend service for its core functionality.

## Third Parties

Thock Studio does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.
