# KeyThock Privacy Policy

Effective date: TODO

KeyThock is designed as a local Mac keyboard sound utility. It plays local sounds in response to keyboard events and does not collect personal data.

## Data Collection

KeyThock does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

KeyThock does not store typed text, passwords, words, sentences, clipboard contents, screenshots, or document contents.

## Keyboard Events

KeyThock requests macOS Input Monitoring permission so it can detect keyboard events and play local keyboard sounds while you type in other apps.

Keyboard events are used only for local sound playback, per-app sound recipes, and optional private counters such as the typed-character countdown. They are not sent to a server and are not used to reconstruct what you typed.

Live key identities from typing are not logged or stored after playback. User-created per-key sound assignments are stored locally only when you configure them in the app. The typed-character countdown stores only a remaining number, not the characters you typed.

## Local Storage

KeyThock stores app settings locally on your Mac, including selected sound pack, volume, mixer preferences, mute settings, per-app sound recipes, focus timer settings, typed-character countdown settings, and per-key sound assignments.

If you import a sound pack, KeyThock copies that user-selected pack into local Application Support storage on your Mac.

## Network Activity

KeyThock does not require an account and does not use a backend service for its core functionality.

## Third Parties

KeyThock does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.
